Lasagna

Description

A pasta dish with layers of of pasta, meat sauce, ricotta, and baked to melty, cheesy perfection.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • packet of taco seasoning
  • shredded lettuce
  • shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • taco shells or corn tortillas

Directions

  1. Cook your ground beef with the taco seaoning until cooked.
  2. put beef in the shell, then put your toppings on it.
